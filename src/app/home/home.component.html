The story to the game

 Since the dawn of mankind there was always a need to settle quarrels a certain way. Once a debate reached a dead end, the settlement often involved physical action and therefore the usage of items of a wide variety. One of the first tools in this context was the good old rock, whose pragmatic usage is known throughout history. While this is quite efficient, it is not always an appropriate solution to come to a consent. The invention of law and scripture led to the usage of paper in order to sign in petitions to solve a dispute in an official manner. A less violent but time taking approach, often stressing the patience of the parties involved. Certain cases took so long that natures way interfered and one could get killed by poisonous lizards in the process. No wonder opponents often resigned to household items like scissors, especially in private disagreements. Now legend has it that one day a man named Spock, from a far evolved future, came to see the barbarism of the contending culture of those times and invented the concept of gaming instead of physical argumentation. The classic Rock, Paper, Scissors was born. While the actual items involved in this game changed through the ages, those variants often lack in balance of power in the game. The version available here is one of the most accepted, and thanks to technological advancements, even physical presents is no longer needed to settle a discord.

Have fun, stay save and get on with it.
